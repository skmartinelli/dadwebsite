The Author

Teacher by day.
Storyteller always.

Matthew A. Martinelli is an author and educator based in Southern California. A high-school Economics teacher, he has been writing fiction since the early 2000s.

His work moves through suspense, horror, supernatural fiction, adventure, and science fiction—often exploring the moments when ordinary lives collide with something unexpected.

His short-story collection Past, Future, Tense was first published in 2014, with a second edition released in 2020.
